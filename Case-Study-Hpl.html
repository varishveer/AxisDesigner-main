Exhibition & Events
  • Phase  1:

    Client Brief with project objective:

    A design brief is an important document that outlines a design project so that the designers and as well as client understand exactly what to expect in terms of deliverables and project workflow. At this stage, clients provide us their goals and ideas for the exhibition which help us to develop requirements and schematic design.

  • Phase  2:

    Concept development

    Detailed design is the phase where the design is refined in terms of functionality, visual look, specifications, and estimates are created. Detailed design will include the development of 2D and 3D models, cost estimation of build-up, procurement plans, etc. This phase is where the full cost of the project is identified and discussed with the client which gives them a clear image regarding their booth before the execution stage.

  • Phase  3:

    Design Development and detailing

    We as a design & build studio in this stage focus on fabrication and installation processes along with hospitality services of the exhibition. In the process, detailed design drawings package issues to the factory to start the fabrication which prepares the booth in parts under the supervision of our design managers. Before the day of the exhibition people from the fabrication and design team install the exhibition booth on site. The aesthetics of design & build components and processes need precision for installation which are taken care of by the design manager and handed over to the client team.
